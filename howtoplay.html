Instructions

GAME 1

Welcome to Dodge the opps! In this game, you control a character who needs to jump over incoming blocks to score points.

Controls:

  • Space Bar: Jump over blocks.
  • G Key: Activate Super Jump (if available).

Gameplay:

  • Each successful jump over a block increases your score.
  • If you collide with a block, the game ends.
  • Collect power-ups to enable a super jump by pressing the G key.
  • Keep an eye on the power-up bar to know when the super jump is available again.
  • Your highest score is saved and displayed as the high score.
